My Bio

Hey! I'm a teen kid who likes anything tech, one of which involves making wallpapers. Also, I enjoy 3D Modelling, so check out my Makerworld Profile if you want to see that. Also, I coded this website myself in HTML and CSS!

Some of them take a lot of time, and some of them take a little time to make.
